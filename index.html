01
Structure

Earthquake-resistant RCC framed structure with lightweight red brick walls offers both strength and durability for long-term stability.

02
Plastering

Quality cement plastering for all internal walls and ceilings, and double-coat sponge finish for exterior surfaces, ensures perfect smoothness.

03
Flooring

Spacious interiors feature 2x4 ft vitrified tiles while all stairs, corridors, toilets, and utility areas have slip-resistant, premium finishes.

04
Kitchen

Modern kitchens come ready for water purifier, chimney, hob, black top counter and SS sink, with glazed tile dado for style and hygiene.

05
Doors & Windows

UPVC windows with MS grills, elegant main and internal doors in teak wood frames, and high-quality hardware ensure beauty and security.

06
Railing

All staircases and balconies are finished with durable MS railings, adding safety and a refined appearance throughout the project.

07
Electrical

Concealed copper wiring and premium branded switches ensure reliable, long-lasting electrical performance and effortless daily living.

08
Painting

Interior walls and ceilings finished with smooth putty and premium Asian Paints emulsion; exteriors coated for long-lasting elegance.

09
Sanitary

Top brands like Jaquar, Parryware, Ashirvad, Sudhakar, Prince, and Finolex are chosen for all CP, CPVC and PVC sanitary ware and piping.

10
Power Backup

Generator backup provided, keeping all common services running seamlessly even during extended power outages for complete peace of mind.

11
Lifts

Two high-capacity, branded 8-passenger lifts ensure fast, reliable and comfortable access for all residents to every apartment floor.

12
Security

All common areas are under constant CCTV surveillance, ensuring a safe, secure, and peaceful living environment for every family.

13
Amenities

Project features badminton and basketball courts, jogging track, sand pit, amphitheatre, multipurpose hall, open gym, and EV charging.
