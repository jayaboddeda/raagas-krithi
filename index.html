About

Our Chairman

The founder and chairman of 9KRAFTSHOMES is a visionary leader with more than 25 years of engineering and infrastructure development experience. where his creative and effective infrastructure project management won him praise.

Our Chairman has a strong track record of success in the real estate sector, having previously worked with prestigious companies like L&T and GMR. He has a degree in Civil Engineering from the National Institute of Technology (NIT) in Warangal and an MBA from the Indian School of Business (ISB) in Hyderabad. Since entering the real estate market five years ago, he has played a key role in locating and seizing important growth prospects as well as in acquiring sizable land pools throughout Hyderabad. His success has been largely attributed to his foresight, which has made him a prominent player in the real estate sector.
